Girls'room

This website is for teenage girls. Adolescent girls can get advice on how to deal with the problems they face. For example: age-related changes in the body and skin, anxiety... Adolescents who do not always share their problems with others are more likely to be depressed because they do not know how to deal with their problems. To prevent this, the goal is to help girls, even if only a little.
